FRONTIER RESTORATION — WEBSITE REQUIREMENTS
===========================================

1. ABOVE-THE-FOLD FIRST IMPRESSION (HOME PAGE HERO)
---------------------------------------------------
Goal: Capture attention in the first 3 seconds and drive immediate calls.

Required Elements:
- Bold hero photo showing water, mold, fire damage, or crew in action
- Primary headline:
  "Fairbanks’ Fast, Trusted Restoration Experts"
- Sub-headline:
  "24/7 Emergency Response • Licensed • IICRC Certified"
- Primary Call-to-Action (CTA) buttons:
  - Call Now (907) 888-8888
  - Request Inspection


2. SERVICES OVERVIEW (SCANNABLE CATEGORIES)
-------------------------------------------
Services must be broken into clean, organized categories with:
- Strong service headline
- Short, clear description
- Real photos of crew or jobs
- CTA button: "Get Help Now"

A. Mitigation:
- Water Damage Mitigation
- Structural Drying (IICRC S500)
- Mold Remediation (IICRC S520)
- Sewage Backup Cleanup

B. Restoration / Rebuild:
- Drywall, Trim, Flooring
- Painting
- Cabinet & Finish Work

C. Specialty Services:
- Air Quality Testing
- Microbial Growth Inspections
- Fire & Smoke Damage
- Ozone Deodorization
- Content Cleaning


3. TRUST BUILDERS & SOCIAL PROOF
--------------------------------
Trust is critical for conversion.

Required:
- Before & After gallery
- Client testimonials
- CompanyCam job gallery integration
- Google reviews (live widget)
- Certification badges:
  - IICRC
  - EPA Lead
  - Contractor License
- Insurance-friendly language:
  "We work directly with all insurance carriers."


4. LOCAL FAIRBANKS AUTHORITY & GEO-TARGETING
--------------------------------------------
Website must clearly reflect local expertise.

Required:
- Service area coverage:
  "Serving Fairbanks, North Pole, Eielson, Fort Wainwright, & surrounding areas"
- References to:
  - Permafrost challenges
  - Sub-zero humidity impacts
- Alaska-specific visuals:
  - Company trucks
  - Local crew
  - Fairbanks landmarks


5. EMERGENCY RESPONSE SECTION
------------------------------
Designed for high-stress emergency users.

Required:
- "What to do right now" checklist
- Response time guarantee
- Call Now button pinned on screen (mobile + desktop)
- Emergency response process outline:
  1. Immediate Phone Assessment
  2. On-site Within __ Hours
  3. Moisture Mapping
  4. Extraction & Drying
  5. Photo Documentation for Insurance


6. ABOUT US PAGE (HUMAN + PROFESSIONAL)
--------------------------------------
Show who the company really is.

Must include:
- Company background
- Mission & Core Values:
  - Honesty
  - Integrity
  - Respect
  - Accountability
- Photos of the Frontier Restoration team
- Story of how the company was built locally


7. HIGH-CONVERSION CONTACT PAGE
--------------------------------
Make it frictionless for customers to reach out.

Required:
- 24/7 phone number
- Business email
- Physical address
- Simple contact form
- Service area map
- Social media links


8. EDUCATIONAL BLOG / RESOURCE CENTER
-------------------------------------
Used for SEO and authority building.

Example blog topics (Alaska-specific):
- How Cold Weather Affects Water Mitigation in Fairbanks
- Signs of Microbial Growth in Cold Climate Homes
- Humidity Control Tips for Alaskan Winters
- Insurance Claim Checklist for Water Damage


9. INSURANCE NAVIGATION & EDUCATION
-----------------------------------
Help clients understand and trust the claims process.

Must include:
- Step-by-step insurance guide
- Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
- Explanation of documentation methods:
  - Photos
  - Moisture readings
  - Psychrometrics
  - Xactimate estimates
- Clear statement:
  "We work directly with adjusters so you don’t have to."


10. MOBILE-FIRST PERFORMANCE & UX
----------------------------------
Majority of users are on phones.

Requirements:
- Fast load times
- Clear, visible buttons
- Large, legible text
- Easy tap-to-call functionality


11. SEARCH ENGINE OPTIMIZATION (SEO)
-----------------------------------
Target keywords:
- Water damage Fairbanks
- Mold remediation Fairbanks
- Fire damage cleanup Fairbanks
- Restoration contractor Fairbanks

Technical SEO:
- Structured data (Local Business schema)
- Optimized page titles & meta descriptions


12. STRONG, LOCAL BRANDING
-------------------------
Brand must feel legitimate, professional, and local.

Requirements:
- Frontier Restoration color scheme
- High-quality photos of real jobs
- Trucks, uniforms, and logos featured
- Clean, blue-collar professional design aesthetic

Overall Goal:
When users land on this site they should immediately think:
"This is a real, trusted, local restoration company."
